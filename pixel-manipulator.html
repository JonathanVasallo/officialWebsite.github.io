Manipulate Pictures

Download on GitHub

Summary
Don't want to pay ridiculous amounts for Photoshop?Your budget solution has arrived!The Pixel Manipulation program can take in any picture of the file types; JPG, JPEG, and PNG. Choose from 5 Filters to apply to your selected image. Each filter is different and can be switched out for another instantly. Once you have decide which filter is the one for you, save it in the blink of an eye.
Functionality
To bring your image into the program...Select the 'Load Picture' button at the top of your Graphical User Interface (GUI).Once selected you will be prompted to choose a file from your directory. Locate your image file, select it, and import it. Once you have successfully loaded the image, at the top menu you will see a drop down menu.Select the arrowThen Choose the filter of your choiceFiltersNormal - Same exact image you uploaded.Grey Scaled Inverted Colors - reversed color scheme based on color valuesMirrored Image - Mirrored Image across the Y-AxisBlack and White Blurred ImageLastly you can either select the...'Save Image' button to name your file and store it'Load Picture' to restart the process\'End Program' to close successfully.
Goal
Provide the user a quick but effective use of filter applications
